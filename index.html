Through out my photography journey, I have captured countless moments that tell unique stories. Each photograph is a window into a memory that will last forever.

The Ethiopian highlands rise like a painting, with rolling green hills stretching into the horizon. Mist drifts gently over the peaks, giving the land a peaceful, almost magical feel. It’s a place where nature feels untouched and timeless

Addis Ababa hums with life, where modern energy meets deep-rooted history. Bustling streets are filled with the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, while vibrant markets overflow with color and culture. Surrounded by rolling hills, the city glows especially at dusk, when lights flicker on and the rhythm of daily life blends into a warm, unforgettable atmosphere

The beauty of bareedu badiya is in its simplicity and the way it captures the essence of Ethiopian life. The vibrant colors and the expressions on people's faces tell a story of resilience and joy.

Coffee from Ethiopia is rich, aromatic, and deeply rooted in tradition. With its smooth taste and floral notes, every cup feels warm and inviting. More than just a drink, Ethiopian coffee is a symbol of hospitality, often shared in meaningful, cultural ceremonies.
